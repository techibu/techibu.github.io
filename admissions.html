Welcome to Our Admissions Page

Thank you for considering Mayuge Light Secondary School for your education. We offer a comprehensive curriculum, experienced faculty, and a supportive learning environment.

Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with the required documents. You can download the application form below or request a physical copy from our administrative office.

2

Document Verification

Our admissions team will review and verify all submitted documents to ensure they meet our requirements.

3

Entrance Assessment

Qualified applicants will be invited for an entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness for our programs.

4

Interview

Following the assessment, selected candidates and their parents/guardians will be invited for an interview with our administrative team.

5

Admission Decision

Based on the assessment and interview results, the admission decision will be communicated to the applicants.

6

Registration

Successful applicants will be invited to complete the registration process, including fee payment and orientation.

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Academic records from previous school (report cards for the last three terms)
  • Leaving certificate from previous school
  • Four passport-sized photographs
  • Immunization records
  • Copy of parent/guardian's national ID

Age Requirements

Students enrolling for Senior One should be between 12 and 14 years of age. For students joining other classes, the age requirement will be considered accordingly.

Academic Requirements

For admission to Senior One, candidates should have successfully completed Primary Seven and performed well in the Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE). For admission to other classes, candidates should have satisfactory academic records from their previous schools.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

Applications are accepted throughout the year. However, for the best chance of admission, we recommend applying at least one month before the start of the term.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer limited scholarships based on academic merit and financial need. Please contact our administration office for more information on scholarship applications.

What are the boarding facilities like?

Our boarding facilities include comfortable dormitories with proper supervision, balanced meals, regular study hours, and various recreational activities.

Do you accept mid-term transfers?

Yes, we accept mid-term transfers subject to availability. Transfer students must provide academic records from their previous school and may need to undergo an assessment.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a teacher-to-student ratio of approximately 1:30 to ensure quality instruction and individual attention.

How can I schedule a visit to the school?

You can schedule a visit by calling our administrative office at +256751018625 or by sending an email to info@mayugelightss.ac.ug. We offer guided tours for prospective students and their families.

Our Programs

At Mayuge Light Secondary School, we offer a well-rounded curriculum that balances academic excellence with character development and life skills.

📚

O-Level (Senior 1-4)

Our O-Level program follows the Ugandan national curriculum and prepares students for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations.

Mathematics English Biology Chemistry Physics Geography History CRE Agriculture
🧪

A-Level (Senior 5-6)

The A-Level program offers specialized combinations of subjects designed to prepare students for university education and future careers.

Physics Chemistry Biology Mathematics Economics Geography Literature History Divinity
🎭

Co-Curricular Activities

We offer a rich variety of co-curricular activities to develop students' talents, interests, and social skills outside the classroom.

Sports Music & Dance Debate Club Science Club Art Club Drama Environmental Club

A-Level Subject Combinations

Science Combinations:
  • PCM: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • PCB: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • BCM: Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • MEG: Mathematics, Economics, Geography
Arts Combinations:
  • HEG: History, Economics, Geography
  • HED: History, Economics, Divinity
  • GED: Geography, Economics, Divinity
  • LAG: Literature, Art, Geography

All combinations include General Paper and Computer Studies as subsidiary subjects.

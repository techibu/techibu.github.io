Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our school

What are the school fees and payment options?

Our fee structure varies by grade level and boarding status. We offer flexible payment options including termly and installment plans. For detailed information about our current fees, please contact our admissions office directly. We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and direct cash payments at our accounts office.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents for admission include:

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school reports (last 2 terms)
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) results (for S1 admission)
  • Transfer letter from previous school (for students transferring)

What subjects are offered at O-Level and A-Level?

At O-Level, we offer all core subjects required by the Uganda National Curriculum including Mathematics, English, Sciences, Social Studies, and various electives. At A-Level, we offer subject combinations in sciences, arts, and humanities. Visit our Academics page for a complete list of subjects offered or contact our academic office for specific information.

Are there transport services available for day scholars?

Yes, we provide transport services for day scholars in selected areas around Mayuge and nearby communities. Our buses operate on established routes with designated pickup and drop-off points. Transport fees vary based on distance. Please contact our administration office for routes, schedules, and fees.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including:

  • Sports (football, netball, volleyball, athletics, cricket)
  • Music, dance, and drama
  • Debate and public speaking
  • Science and mathematics clubs
  • Environmental club
  • Creative arts
  • Religious organizations
  • Community service opportunities

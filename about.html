Our Story

From humble beginnings to a center of educational excellence in the region

Building a Legacy of Excellence

Mayuge Light Secondary School was founded in 2009 with a vision to provide quality and affordable education to the community. From humble beginnings with just a few classrooms and a handful of dedicated teachers, we have grown into a premier educational institution in the region.

Over the years, our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education has not wavered. We've continually invested in our infrastructure, teaching staff, and educational resources to provide our students with the best possible learning environment.

Today, Mayuge Light Secondary School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, producing graduates who excel in both academic pursuits and as responsible citizens making positive contributions to society.

Mission, Vision & Values

The guiding principles that define who we are and what we stand for

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for academic excellence and personal growth in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of academic excellence that nurtures responsible, innovative, and compassionate global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all endeavors, pushing boundaries and exceeding expectations in academics, character, and service.

Integrity

We uphold high ethical standards and honesty in all our interactions and decisions, cultivating a culture of trust and accountability.

Respect

We value the dignity, diversity, and unique contributions of every individual in our community, fostering an inclusive and supportive environment.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and innovative approaches to teaching and learning, preparing students for the challenges of the future.

Leadership

We develop leadership skills in our students, preparing them to lead with purpose, compassion, and a commitment to positive change.

Community Service

We promote a spirit of giving back to the community and making a positive impact on society through meaningful service and engagement.
